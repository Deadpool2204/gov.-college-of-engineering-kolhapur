About Us

Government College of Engineering, Kolhapur was established in 1961 and has grown to become one of the leading engineering institutes in Maharashtra. Our mission is to provide world-class technical education and research opportunities.

Our Vision

1. To nurture technically sound professionals with global competence.

2. To be a center of excellence in engineering education.

3. To drive innovation through cutting-edge research and development.

Our Mission

1. Providing quality education and fostering an environment conducive to learning.

2. Promoting research and innovation to solve real-world problems.

3. Developing well-rounded individuals who can contribute positively to society.
