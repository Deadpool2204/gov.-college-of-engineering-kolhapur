Admissions

We are excited to welcome new students to our college. Our admission process is straightforward and designed to help us find the best candidates for our programs.

  • Undergraduate Admissions: Based on performance in entrance exams and previous academic records. We also consider extracurricular achievements.
  • Postgraduate Admissions: Includes an entrance test, an interview, and academic credentials. We seek candidates with strong research interests and academic excellence.
  • Application Deadlines: Typically in June for undergraduate programs and November for postgraduate programs. Please check our website for exact dates and further details.
