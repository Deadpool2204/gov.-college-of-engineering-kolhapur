Our Programs

At Government College of Engineering, Kolhapur, we offer a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in their careers.

Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

This program delves into the latest advancements in AI and data analytics, preparing students for roles in this rapidly evolving field.

Computer Science

Focuses on software engineering, computing fundamentals, and advanced technologies, equipping students for a range of tech-related careers.

Electrical Engineering

Offers a comprehensive understanding of electrical systems, automation, and energy management, with a focus on practical applications.

Electronics & Telecommunication

Covers communication systems, electronics, and network technologies, preparing students for careers in telecommunications and electronics.

Mechanical Engineering

Provides in-depth knowledge of mechanical design, manufacturing processes, and thermodynamics, with hands-on experience in mechanical systems.
